About us

Fookery has been created by a team that is extremely passionate about food, wine and all things fine. As a collective group of Chefs and Hoteliers who have worked in some of the best hotels and restaurants around the world, we were always on the lookout for new dining experiences and exciting cuisines. It is this love and passion for eating and exploring that brought us together to create Fookery.
How often do you find yourself wondering, where you should go eat on a special occasion? What are the newest and top eateries in town? What are the things you absolutely must try when visiting a particular restaurant? Can you save some money while still dining at top restaurants of the city?
Fookery has all the answers for the most enjoyable, authentic and friction-free table booking experience. With an instant confirmation of less than 20 seconds, you can now book over 10,000+ restaurants in India on our swanky website or mobile app. For bookings in India, we also provide a 24 x 7 complimentary concierge service.
With every meal you book through Fookery, there comes a phenomenal deal ranging from free alcoholic drinks to up to 50% off on your total bill. With no membership fees or reservations charge, what is best is that these eye-popping deals are not for just one person, but are applicable on the entire table! Now, this is what I call a real deal!
Fookery is evolving each day to become your daily dining app by providing pre-paid discounted meals and vouchers, including upto 50% off at Chaayos, Burger King and many others! If we had our way, these prepaid meals would be the only gifts our families would be giving us!
With exclusive and expert reviews by top critics helmed by our co-founder, the iconic Kartik Singhal, Fookery serves as a curated guide to an extensive range of dining options and is your reliable guide to eating out. 
With each booking, you also earn FookeryPoints, which can be redeemed for an array of delicious treats. You earn points for booking through Fookery as well as by leaving user reviews on restaurant profiles. The more points you earn, the more delicious and exciting the rewards become!
Do take the time to #Thank - an initiative by Fookery which allows you to acknowledge the efforts of your servers and chefs at the restaurant and help them win amazing rewards while you get 20 Fookery Points for every review. It’s a total win-win.
If you're looking for large group bookings of more than 10 guests, suggestions, ideas or recommendations on eating out; just call our  24 hour Fookery Concierge at 9811573286 all 7 days a week!
Take the smoothly way out and become a FookeryDine for life!

FAQs:

1. What is FookeryDine Prime?
Ans: Fookery Dine Prime promises exclusive access to the hottest bars and restaurants and guarantees you a minimum 25%, and up to 50% off at top restaurants in India. Select a suitable membership plan (1-Years/6-Months/1-Months) and get guaranteed discounts at 2000+ premium restaurants along with VIP access across India.
2. How do I get free FookeryDine Prime?
Ans: FookeryDine Prime is a paid-only membership. Keep checking the FookeryDine app for latest offers and limited-time FREE memberships.
3. How do I cancel my FookeryDine Prime membership?
Ans: You can initiate cancellation of your FookeryDine Prime membership anytime; however, the membership will be active till the subscription period, post which it will remain cancelled.
4. How do I upload my bill to FookeryDine?
Ans: Once you’ve dined through FookeryDine, you will get an option to ‘Upload your Bill’. Follow the steps and just make sure that the bill images are clear along with the proper bill number, restaurant name, date, time and amount. Handwritten bills or manual entries will not be accepted.
5. How do I redeem FookeryDine points?
Ans: Earn FookeryPoints with every reservation and redeem them for Prime memberships, a complimentary meal for two or a hotel stay!
6. How do I cancel an FookeryDine booking?
Ans: You can cancel your booking anytime in your profile section through the app, website or simply by calling the Fookery Concierge at 9811573286. FookeryDine has no cancellation charges.
7. How do I use FookeryDine?
Ans: You can use FookeryDine by visiting www.fookery.com or download its mobile app on Android/iOS devices.
8. What restaurants have the best deals?
Ans: Restaurants listed on FookeryDine provide you with top-notch deals. Get minimum guaranteed 25% up to 50% OFF at FookeryDine Prime-enabled restaurants
